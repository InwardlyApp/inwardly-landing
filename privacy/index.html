Privacy Policy

Effective: March 14, 2026

1. Your Privacy Matters

Inwardly is built on a simple principle: your journal is yours alone. We designed every aspect of this app with your privacy as the top priority.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and an optional display name. This is used solely for authentication and personalizing your experience.

Journal Entries

Your journal entries, mood scores, location labels, and photos are stored in our database. All entry content is encrypted with AES-256-CTR before being stored. Your encryption key is generated on your device and stored in the iOS Keychain — we never have access to it.

Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymized usage data (e.g., feature usage counts, crash reports) to improve the app. This data cannot be linked back to your journal content.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • AI Features: When you opt into AI insights, your encrypted entries are temporarily decrypted on-device and sent to our secure AI proxy. The AI provider (Anthropic) does not store or train on your data.
  • Notifications: To send you optional journaling reminders (you control these)
  • Subscriptions: To manage your premium subscription via RevenueCat

4. What We Never Do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Show you advertisements
  • Train AI models on your writing
  • Share your journal content with anyone
  • Access your encryption key

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring you can only access your own data. All connections use TLS encryption in transit. Entry content is AES-256-CTR encrypted at rest.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings within the app. Deletion is permanent and irreversible. When you delete your account, we remove all entries, photos, journals, templates, and account information from our systems.

7. Data Export

You can export all your journal entries at any time via the Data Export feature in Settings. Exports are available in JSON and plain text formats.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Anthropic (Claude): AI insights and pattern analysis (data is not stored or used for training)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple: App distribution and in-app purchases

9. Children's Privacy

Inwardly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@goinwardly.app
