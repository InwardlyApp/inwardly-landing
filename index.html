Features

Everything you need to
reflect & grow

Daily Journaling

Write freely with AI-generated prompts that adapt to your mood and patterns. Track location, photos, and emotions.

AI Insights

Discover emotional patterns and receive thoughtful weekly reviews powered by Claude AI. Your personal reflection partner.

Streaks & Stats

Build a writing habit with daily streaks, word counts, and monthly emotional trend reports. Watch your consistency grow.

Privacy First

AES-256 encryption, no data selling, no ads. Your journal is yours alone. Encryption keys live on your device.

Multiple Journals

Organize your life with separate journals for work, personal, travel, and more. Keep everything in its place.

Templates

Start with structured templates for workouts, productivity, collections, and more. Or create your own custom templates.

Your journal is sacred

Every entry is encrypted with AES-256 before it leaves your device. We never sell your data, serve ads, or train AI models on your writing. Your encryption key lives in your device's secure enclave — not on our servers.
