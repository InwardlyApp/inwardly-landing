Only you. Every entry is encrypted with AES-256 on your device before it's ever uploaded. The encryption key lives in your phone's secure keychain — not on our servers. Even if someone gained access to our database, all they'd see is unreadable ciphertext. When you use AI features, your decrypted text is sent to Claude via a secure proxy and immediately discarded — never stored.

You can read and write journal entries without an internet connection — your entries are stored locally on your device. AI features (weekly reviews, pattern detection, personalized prompts) require a connection since they call Claude. Your entries sync to the cloud automatically when you reconnect.
